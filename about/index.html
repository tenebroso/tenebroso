About Tenebroso

Tenebroso is the work of Jon Bukiewicz, a WordPress Customization Expert & Designer from Chicago with over 18 years of experience.

Website Design since 1996.

How did I get started making websites? Since sitting down with my Dad to learn some of the basics as a kid back in 1996, my interest in web design has continually grown and expanded. I began in the days of non-transparent .gifs and Netscape Navigator (Geocities, anyone?) by working on official websites for nationally known bands including The Insyderz, Squad Five-O and Johnny Respect. From there, I maintained a growing interest in web design through blogging both as a photographer and a writer while in high school. This led to an exploration of content management & blogging systems like Blogger & WordPress, visual enhancement & usability through jQuery, and valid HTML, XHTML & CSS through such talented trailblazers as the folks behind A List Apart, CSS Tricks and many others.

It is my hands-on experience combined with an ability to quickly adapt to new web technologies that has kept my interest in web design sharp. Currently I hold a position as the primary Midwest Regional Web Developer for an international Non-Profit Organization. It is here that I have further advanced in my working knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Fireworks, Illustrator, XHTML & HTML5 coding, jQuery, CSS2.1 & CSS3, Server Optimization & more. Tenebroso Web & Graphic design fuels my desire to also keep sharp on WordPress, PHP and customized media development.

WordPress Customization is my Bread & Butter

I live in Chicago with my wife and our two wonderful kids. We try to inspire positive change around us, where we are, by making environmentally and socially creative choices with our resources and time.
